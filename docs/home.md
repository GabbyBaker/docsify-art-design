## Learning Paths

### [Giving Glory: Art & Discipleship](cpt363-1/home.md)

Music, fashion, film -- the creative arts arena has been taken over by so much that draws minds away from Christ. This course explores the great controversy in the digital arts space and how illustrative art can be a power for the gospel.

### [Getting Into Digital Art](cpt363-2/home.md)
Drawing on a computer? Digital art can be like stepping onto a new planet. This course will guide you through what it takes to become a digital artist, including tools, techniques, and testimonials.

## Why Use Layers?

When we create illustrations on paper or other physical medium, every change, stroke, or color goes right on top of the things beneath in. But this means that when we look back at the completed project, we have no idea which change happened second or fourth, and we have no way of modifying a single change without affecting other aspects of the art.

In the digital world, this loss of information and control is <u>tragic</u>.

For digital work, it is important to use version control and **nondestructive editing**. How we save and organize our files helps us keep track of version control; using layers and masks helps us edit nondestructively.
